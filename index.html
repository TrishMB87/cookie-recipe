Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Delicious and easy to make!

EASY CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIE RECIPE With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions, you’ll have warm homemade chocolate chip cookies fresh from the oven in no time. Freeze the cookie dough for later use or bake all at once for a homemade treat. Try enhancing the flavor with sea salt, experiment with an oat flour version or celebrate with a cookie cake. You can’t go wrong with this classic recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugars with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture and mix well.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips by hand and drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  6. Bake 11-13 minutes or until edges are golden brown. Cool 3 minutes on pan; remove and cool completely on cooling grid.
